About Me

Pop Pistachio is a Bronx-born, Las Vegas-based music producer who brings the raw energy of East Coast hip hop to the neon lights of Sin City. Drawing inspiration from the rich musical tapestry of the Bronx and the eclectic vibrancy of Las Vegas, Pop Pistachio masterfully blends urban grit with cutting-edge beats.

Self-taught and ever-experimenting, Pop Pistachio crafts tracks that are as bold and intricate as their artistic vision. Their music captures the contrasts of their surroundings, creating sounds that are both thought-provoking and electrifying.

In their free time, Pop Pistachio finds inspiration in the dynamic landscapes around them and enjoys collaborating with others to share their unique musical vision.
